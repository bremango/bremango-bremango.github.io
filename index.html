about myself

Welcome to my creative space! As a second-year student in Global Business and Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo, I strive to use my artistic skills while adding to my skill set along the way. I have been experienced in InDesign, Photoshop, and Illustrator since the start of high school through the Cyber Arts program. This program focuses on both traditional and digital art. With the passion to express my art through both styles, I can continue to expand my designs through jobs and in my personal life. Learn more about my experience in my resume and artist catalogue.
